Getting Started

How do I set up my first AI voice agent?

Setting up your first AI agent is simple. Sign up for an account, choose your implementation plan (Basic, Business, or Premium), and our team will guide you through the setup process.

What information do I need to provide?

You'll need your business information, call scripts or conversation flows, integration requirements (CRM, phone systems), and preferred voice characteristics.

How long does implementation take?

Basic setup can be done in 1-2 weeks. Business plans typically take 2-4 weeks, and Premium plans vary based on complexity.

Do I need technical knowledge?

No technical knowledge required for Basic and Business plans. Our team handles all technical setup. Premium plans include comprehensive support.

Can I customize the AI voice?

Yes! All plans include some level of voice customization. Business and Premium plans include custom voice training for your brand.

Billing & Pricing

How does the monthly billing work?

You're billed monthly based on your call minute usage. The implementation fee is a one-time charge for setup and configuration.

Can I change my plan anytime?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

What happens if I exceed my minutes?

If you exceed your monthly minutes, you'll be billed for the additional usage at your current rate. We'll notify you when you're approaching your limit.

Is there a free trial available?

We offer a 14-day free trial for Business plans. Contact our sales team to start your trial.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and wire transfers for Enterprise accounts.
